Cherry Pie

Description

Cherry Pie is the holy grail of all pies. There are thousands of cherry pie recipes on the internet. But what sets our recipe apart is that ours is created as a homage to the famous 90's show, Twin Peaks. In the world of Twin Peaks, Norma's Cherry Pie is the tastiest sweet ever, and it is a favorite of both the townies and outsiders. When you taste our Cherry Pie, you will feel as if you are in Norma's R&R. We highly suggest you to eat it alongside a "damn good cup of coffee."

Ingredients

For the filling

  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 3 tbsp Cornflour
  • 850g cherries, de-stoned
  • 110g sugar
  • 3tbsp water
  • 1/2 tbsp almond essence

For the pastry

  • 80ml water
  • 100g butter
  • 100g vegetable shortening
  • 300g plain flour
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • sugar for dusting

Steps

  1. Make the filling by stirring the ingredients in a large bowl. Cover and place in refrigerator for up to 24 hours.
  2. Roll out the dough.
  3. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  4. Arrange the lattice.
  5. Brush the top of the pie crust with the egg wash.
  6. Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes. Turn the temperature down a bit and bake for 40 minutes more until the top is golden brown.
  7. Remove from oven and cool for 3-4 hours before slicing and serving.
